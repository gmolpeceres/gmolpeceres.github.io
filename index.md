# Germán Molpeceres

## <span class="hero-title">Molecular Astrophysicist & Astrochemist</span>

Welcome to the webmage of Germán Molpeceres. I am an astrochemist based in Madrid working in the study of the emergence of chemical complexity in the interstellar medium from a theoretical approach, combining electronic structure calculations, theoretical modeling and astrochemical modeling.
